Dos Palos, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Dos Palos (Spanish for 'Two Timbers') is a city in southern Merced County, California, United States. Dos Palos is located 27 miles (43 km) south-southwest of Merced, the county seat, at an elevation of 118 feet (36 m). The population was 5,798 at the 2020 census, up from 4,950 at the 2010 census.
